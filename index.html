About Us

Your Trusted Partner for
Industrial Solutions

Veemax Solutions, based in Hyderabad, is a manufacturer and authorised multi-brand dealer for industrial process instrumentation, control systems and electrical products. We serve power, chemical, pharma, oil & gas and food processing industries.

We manufacture custom FLP Thermocouples, RTDs, Thermowells and Electrical Heaters to exact client drawings. We supply and install MCC Panels, Fire Suppression Systems, FRP Blowers and complete instrumentation packages.
